about arc-fm

Weclome to the Archive of Free Music, also known as ARC-FM. We're a site that draws from FMA, a free music service. We took the data from FMA's API and collected it in a much more simple, robust way. Explore. Enjoy.

clean data

We took as much data as FMA was willing to give us, and packaged it in a nice way. Thanks to our sanitized data, your computer nor our service is at risk from trolls.

clean music

We know some of you are tired of hearing roughed up music. So, we filtered through the FMA API and exlude from ARC-FM those tracks that can be jarring. Listen in peace. (If you're looking for that kinda thing, well, our bad!)

clean design

ARC-FM is polished. We took time to make it nice, iron out the wrinkles, and create a streamlined UI. We hope you enjoy it as much as we do.
